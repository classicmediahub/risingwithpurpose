About the Book

A Wake-Up Call to a Generation Full of Potential

Rising with Purpose, Power & Influence is not just a book. It is a wake-up call to a generation that is full of potential but sometimes confused about direction. Written for teenagers and young adults who know deep inside that there is more to life than just going to school, getting a job, and surviving — for those who feel called to something greater but are still figuring out what that "greater" really is.

In these pages, you will discover that purpose is not something you stumble into accidentally — it is something you grow into. You will understand that power is not about control or dominance, but about discipline, character, and inner strength. And you will learn that influence is not reserved for celebrities or leaders; every young person already carries influence, whether they realize it or not.

This book breaks down the mountains that shape society — education, faith, leadership, media, and more — and shows how young people can rise within them without losing their identity. It is practical. It is spiritual but balanced. It is honest. It is real.
